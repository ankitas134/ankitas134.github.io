Wind Forecasting

ICTEAH 2026 · PAPER ID 172

Physics-Informed Feature Engineering: Enhancing Short-Term Wind Power Forecasting

Anjnney Salvi · Aarushee Pandagare · Ankita Survase

Introduces three physics-derived features — Wind Power Density (cubic law), Turbulence Intensity (rolling σ), and Lagged Efficiency Proxy — engineered from raw SCADA sensor data. Applied to 50,530 records across four ML models under a strict chronological 80:20 split. Physics-XGBoost achieves state-of-the-art accuracy with zero look-ahead bias. Presented at CiiA-5 Innovation Exhibition, Nehru Science Centre, Mumbai.
